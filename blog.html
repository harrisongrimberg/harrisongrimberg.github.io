Let’s Make Juice Time Awesome!
Posted on April 21st 2025 by Harrison Grimberg

At Penny Juice, our mission has always been clear: to provide young children with a healthier, more exciting way to stay hydrated. We believe that early childhood is the most important time to build good habits, and what kids drink is just as important as what they eat. That’s why we’ve created a line of colorful, flavorful juices made from 100% fruit juice concentrate, without added sugars, preservatives, or artificial colors. Our goal is to offer a beverage option that supports growing bodies, encourages better nutrition, and makes snack time fun — because when healthy choices taste great, kids naturally reach for them.

Focusing on kids also means focusing on the people who care for them — childcare centers, preschools, and early learning programs that need reliable, affordable options they can trust. We designed Penny Juice to be easy to prepare, cost-effective, and packed with flavors kids love, making it the perfect fit for busy classrooms and daycare snack times. By putting kids first, we’re helping to build healthier futures, one cup at a time — and that's something we’re proud to be part of every day.
